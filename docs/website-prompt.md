**Style Fusion:** Warm, artisanal authenticity blended with modern/minimalist layout

**Visual Strategy:**
- **Color Palette:** 
  - Warm wood tones (natural parquet colors: golden oak, walnut, ash)

- **Typography:**

  - High-quality, professionally edited photos of installed parquet

- "Wooden flooring installation Lazio"
- "Professional parquet installer Rome"
- "Doors installation Rome"
- "Wood expert Lazio"

**On-Page SEO:**
- **Meta titles:** Keyword + brand (55-60 chars)
  - Example: "Parquet Installation Rome | 20+ Years Expert Craftsmanship"
- **Meta descriptions:** Compelling, include CTA, location (155-160 chars)
  - Example: "Premium parquet flooring installation in Rome & Lazio. 20+ years expertise. Free consultation. Call today for perfect wooden floors."
- **H1-H6 Structure:** Proper hierarchy on each page
- **Image alt text:** Descriptive, keyword-relevant
  - Example: "Professional parquet installation before and after in Rome apartment"
- **URL structure:** Clean, keyword-relevant

  - /portfolio/
  - /contatti/

**Technical SEO:**
- Mobile-responsive (critical)
- Fast loading (optimize images)
- Clean code, structured data (schema markup)
- Sitemap + robots.txt
- Google My Business optimization (Rome location)

**Local SEO:**
- Google My Business listing (name, address, phone, hours)
- Local citations (Italian business directories)
- Local reviews (encourage clients to review on Google)
